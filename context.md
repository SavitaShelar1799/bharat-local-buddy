# Mumbai Local Context Guide

## City Overview
Mumbai is a fast-paced Indian metropolitan city where people value efficiency, street-smart advice, and informal communication.

## Language & Slang
- Bhai: Friendly way to address someone
- Tapri: Roadside tea stall
- Cutting: Small cup of tea
- Jugaad: Quick workaround
- Local: Mumbai suburban train
- Timepass: Casual hangout

## Food Culture
- Vada Pav is Mumbai’s most popular street food
- Evening snacks are preferred
- Popular food zones:
  - Dadar: Vada pav, misal
  - Andheri: Rolls, street Chinese
  - Juhu: Pav bhaji, chaat

## Commute Patterns
- Peak hours: 8–11 AM, 5–9 PM
- Local trains are fastest but crowded
- Auto-rickshaws don’t operate in South Mumbai
- Walking short distances is common

## Cultural Behavior
- People prefer short, practical answers
- Luxury recommendations are less appreciated
- Locals respect confidence and clarity
